### üìÑ 1. Visa Violations & Overstaying
Foreigners must strictly adhere to their visa validity.

* **Overstaying:** Staying in India beyond your visa expiry is a serious offense under the **Foreigners Act, 1946**.
* **Penalty:**
    * **Financial Penalty:** Varies based on duration (often $300 - $500 USD).
    * **Legal Action:** You may be detained, deported, and **blacklisted** from entering India again.
    * **Exit Permit:** You cannot just leave the airport; you must apply for an **Exit Permit** online via the FRRO portal before departure.

### üè¢ 2. FRRO Registration (Foreigners Regional Registration Office)
* **Who must register?**
    * Anyone with a **Student Visa**, **Employment Visa**, or **Research Visa**.
    * Anyone with a Tourist Visa valid for **more than 180 days** (if staying continuously for >180 days).
* **Time Limit:** You must register within **14 days** of arrival in India.
* **How to Register:** It is fully digital via the **e-FRRO Portal** (https://indianfrro.gov.in).

### üè® 3. Form C (Hotel Reporting)
* **The Rule:** Every hotel, hostel, guesthouse, or private host (Airbnb) accommodating a foreigner **MUST** submit **Form C** to the FRRO within **24 hours** of the guest's arrival.
* **Your Duty:** You typically don't file this yourself, but you must provide your passport and visa details to your host so they can file it. Failure to do so can lead to fines for both the host and the guest.

### üíº 4. Allowed Activities by Visa Type
* **Tourist Visa:** Strictly for recreation and sightseeing. **Working** or **journalism** is strictly prohibited.
* **Business Visa:** For meetings, sales, and trade. Not for employment (salary cannot be paid in India).
* **Employment Visa:** Required if you are working and earning a salary in India.

* **Strict Ban:** Carrying or using **Satellite Phones** (like Thuraya or Iridium) is **ILLEGAL** in India without specific government permission.
