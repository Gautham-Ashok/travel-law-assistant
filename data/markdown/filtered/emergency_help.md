India has unified emergency numbers that work across all states.

* **Government Hospitals:** Provide low-cost or free emergency care. Look for "Civil Hospital" or "Government Medical College" in your area.
* **Private Hospitals:** Generally offer faster service and better facilities but require payment or insurance.
* **Ambulance:** Dial **108** for a government ambulance. Private hospitals often have their own ambulance numbers.

### ğŸš— 4. Road Accident Protocol (Good Samaritan Law)
If you witness an accident or are involved in one:
* **No Harassment:** Under the **Good Samaritan Guidelines**, bystanders who help accident victims **cannot** be forced to reveal their identity or be detained by the police.
* **Hospital Duty:** Hospitals **must** provide immediate First Aid / stabilization treatment to accident victims without waiting for police clearance (Supreme Court Order).

* **Step 1:** Immediately file a **"Lost Report"** or **FIR (First Information Report)** at the nearest police station. You cannot get a new visa/passport without this police report.
